Dakota Walsh

Starbucks Barista, Barista Trainer, and Coffee Master☕

Hired as a barista in August 2020, I have continued my career here at Starbucks, developing within the company day by day. As a regular barista until January 2022, I gained many skills over two different Starbucks locations. I know almost everything Starbucks related and consider myself very knowledgeable in the Starbucks realm. I enjoyed my job as a regular barista extensively, but was ready for a new challenge within the company. As I hit my year and a half mark with the company, I became a coffee master.

The title of coffee master consists of special training that grants the knowledge of all things coffee. From flavors, to origins, to pairing coffee with the right foods for tasting, I know it all. The title of coffee master is accompanied by a special black Starbucks apron with the title "Coffee Master" embroidered on it. I wear it proudly, however, am now ready for a different endeavor within the company.

Presently, I am working on my barista trainer certification. I have always wanted to be a barista trainer and introduce new employees to the Starbucks culture. I cannot wait to see where this new title takes me and the things I can accomplish in the future!
